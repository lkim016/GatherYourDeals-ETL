## Methodology

Provider calls are replaced with `asyncio.sleep` stubs that draw latency
from lognormal distributions fit to observed P50/P95 values:

| Stage | P50 (ms) | P95 (ms) | μ | σ |
|-------|--------:|--------:|------|------|
| OCR (Azure DI) | 5000 | 11000 | 8.517 | 0.479 |
| LLM (OpenRouter) | 3718 | 10080 | 8.221 | 0.606 |
| LLM (CLOD) | 3509 | 9242 | 8.163 | 0.589 |
| Geocode (Maps) | 500 | 1200 | 6.215 | 0.532 |

The ADI concurrency cap is modelled as `asyncio.Semaphore(15)`. At N ≤ 8 workers this limit is not reached; run with `--workers 1 2 4 8 16` to observe its throttling effect.

Speedup is computed relative to the N=1 (sequential) baseline.
